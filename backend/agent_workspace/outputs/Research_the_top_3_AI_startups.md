## Introduction to AI Startups in India
The Indian AI startup ecosystem is thriving, with a focus on both horizontal and vertical solutions. The key sectors where AI startups are predominantly operating include **fintech**, **healthcare**, **ecommerce**, **education**, and **robotics**.

The top AI startups in India are focused on developing:
- **Horizontal solutions** such as speech-to-text, video generation, and text-based AI
- **Vertical solutions** tailored for specific sectors
Some of the key areas of focus for Indian AI startups include:
- **Enterprise platforms**
- **Healthcare AI solutions**

## Key Findings
The Indian AI startup ecosystem is rapidly growing and competitive globally, with many startups showcasing innovations powered by AI models and building real infrastructure. The use of AI is helping to transform sectors across the economy, and Indian startups are deploying AI with depth and breadth.
